## 📣 Workshop Organised — GlobalFact 2026

Together with [Mohsen Mosleh](https://www.linkedin.com/in/mohsen-mosleh-51824959/) ([Oxford Internet Institute, University of Oxford](https://www.linkedin.com/company/oxford-internet-institute/)), [Naomi Miyashita](https://www.linkedin.com/in/naomi-miyashita-11a77a2/) ([United Nations](https://www.linkedin.com/company/united-nations/) Department of Peace Operations), and [Joseph Aylett-Bullock](https://www.linkedin.com/in/joseph-aylett-bullock-854309105/) ([United Nations](https://www.linkedin.com/company/united-nations/) Department of Peace Operations), we organised a workshop at GlobalFact 2026 / the [International Fact-Checking Network](https://www.linkedin.com/company/factchecknet/) Conference in Vilnius with support from the [Oxford Policy Engagement Network (OPEN)](https://www.linkedin.com/company/oxford-policy-engagement-network/) Seed Fund.

**Designing Evidence-Informed Information Integrity Strategies in High-Risk Contexts**

*Date:* 18 June 2026 · 3:20 PM EET

*Location:* GlobalFact 2026, Vilnius, Lithuania 🇱🇹

This workshop brought together researchers, fact-checkers, and policy stakeholders to explore how evidence can meaningfully inform information integrity strategies in conflict-affected and other high-risk contexts.

We're grateful to the [Oxford Policy Engagement Network (OPEN)](https://www.linkedin.com/company/oxford-policy-engagement-network/) Seed Fund for making this possible.

## 📣 Study Completed — AI in Fact-Checking

*The recruitment phase of this study is now closed. Thank you to everyone who participated!*

We conducted a research study at the **Oxford Internet Institute**, **University of Oxford**, to better understand how fact-checkers use artificial intelligence (AI), especially generative AI tools such as ChatGPT, in their work.

If you would like to receive a summary of the findings once available, feel free to reach out: [basak.bozkurt@oii.ox.ac.uk](mailto:basak.bozkurt@oii.ox.ac.uk)
