--- layout: archive title: "Workshops" permalink: /workshops/ author_profile: true --- {% include base_path %} ## GlobalFact 2026 Workshop **Designing Evidence-Informed Information Integrity Strategies in High-Risk Contexts** Together with [Mohsen Mosleh](https://www.linkedin.com/in/mohsen-mosleh-51824959/) ([Oxford Internet Institute, University of Oxford](https://www.linkedin.com/company/oxford-internet-institute/)), [Naomi Miyashita](https://www.linkedin.com/in/naomi-miyashita-11a77a2/) ([United Nations](https://www.linkedin.com/company/united-nations/) Department of Peace Operations), and [Joseph Aylett-Bullock](https://www.linkedin.com/in/joseph-aylett-bullock-854309105/) ([United Nations](https://www.linkedin.com/company/united-nations/) Department of Peace Operations), we organised this workshop at GlobalFact 2026 / the [International Fact-Checking Network](https://www.linkedin.com/company/factchecknet/) Conference in Vilnius with support from the [Oxford Policy Engagement Network (OPEN)](https://www.linkedin.com/company/oxford-policy-engagement-network/) Seed Fund. *Date:* 18 June 2026 · 3:20 PM EET *Location:* GlobalFact 2026, Vilnius, Lithuania 🇱🇹 This workshop brought together researchers, fact-checkers, and policy stakeholders to explore how evidence can meaningfully inform information integrity strategies in conflict-affected and other high-risk contexts.
